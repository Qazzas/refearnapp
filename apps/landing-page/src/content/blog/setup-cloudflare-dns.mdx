    <p className="text-slate-600 text-lg leading-relaxed">
      The high energy demand remains the Achilles' heel of the vertical farming industry. Because artificial lighting must run between 14 to 18 hours per day to maximize photosynthesis, electricity costs often dwarf labor and seed costs combined.
    </p>
  </section>

  {/* Section 6: Thermal Management */}

    </p>
  </section>
