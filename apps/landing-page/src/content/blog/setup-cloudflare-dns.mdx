Setting up your DNS correctly is the most important step for white-labeling your referral program.

## Step 1: Access your DNS Dashboard
Login to Cloudflare and select your domain. Navigate to the DNS settings tab.

## Why use CNAME records?
CNAME records allow RefearnApp to track clicks on your own domain.
